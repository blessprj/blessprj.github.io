Seattle Residential & Real Estate Photography

Have your home professionally photographed to showcase it to its best advantage and dramatically increase your chances of selling. I work with real estate professionals and homeowners in the Seattle - Puget Sound area, photographing homes both large and small, from suburban ramblers to luxury condos. Options include full interior and exterior photos, twilight images, drone/aerial photography, and 3D Matterport tours.

Interior and Architectural Photography

This type of photography is intended for design professionals and commercial projects - architects, interior designers, design/build companies, and similar professions, where the utmost attention is paid to the smallest detail.

I work with design professionals to carefully compose each shot, lighting the space as needed, while maintaining the natural ambiance of the surroundings. The goal is to create expressive photographs that can be used for promoting the business to its best advantage.

COVID-19

I attempt as much as possible to adhere to social distancing guidelines. Ideally, I prefer to be in the home alone during a shoot, but I understand that is not always practical. If it’s necessary that we be in the home together, working in separate rooms is the best option. I always bring a mask and will wear it when necessary (it fogs up the camera lens, so it can be a challenge).
